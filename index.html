How it works

What do I cook?".

The endless wondering of what to eat is a burden to us all. To be honest, we've all been there a lot of times. The few times we have the time and ingredients to cook, we end up cooking the same meals everyday. What if we could reduce the time and burden of deciding what to eat. That's where we come in.

Introducing Dish-E is a site where you can get access to all kinds of recipes by just a click of a button. Search for what to cook using an ingredient you have and get meal suggestions together with their recipe and instruction video.
