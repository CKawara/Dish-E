Frequently Asked Questions

What does Dish-E mean?
Dish-e is an applicatiion designed to help users decide what to cook based on what they have in their pantry.
Dish-e comes from the word "dishi" which means eat in Kenyan slang.

How do I get a recipe?
Go to the home page enter the name of your main ingredient, hit the search button and choose from a varitey of recommendations

Can I substitute an ingredient?
We recommend using the specified ingredients listed in the recipe for optimal results.
